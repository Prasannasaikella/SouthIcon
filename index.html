About Aadhya Pharmaceuticals and its evolution

During my initial days of graduation, the urge to set up a pharmaceutical company and serve cross section of society by making available quality products at affordable prices was very much in mind. This has come true now after setting up successful Educational institutions, Real Estates and in Hospitality sector. We also have a logistics division which helps in transporting things timely. My aim is to make available our formulations to the remotest places in India over a period of time as some of the under privileged do not have access to medical treatment. I am sure I would be able to achieve this with everyone’s support ,and I am also confident that Aadhya Pharmaceuticals would be a name to reckon with in the coming years.
GUDAPATI SARATH
